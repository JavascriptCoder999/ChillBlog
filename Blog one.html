01. Chillhop Essentials Fall + Winter 2016 vinyl REPRESS

Chillhop Essentials is a set of compilations made every season of the essentials of Chillhop Music. It started in spring 2016 on 04.04, Later in the year, when Fall 2016 came out on 10.10, it was pressed on black vinyl limited to 314 hand numbered copies. In around Fall 2017, I found out about Chillhop, and the 2016 essentials have been my all-time favourite so far, but only recently I have started collecting the records of Chillhop Music. I live in the UK, and I searched up the album on Discogs, and there was only 2 for sale, both had sleeve and media condition near mint, the prices were €300 (£248.73) and €330 (£286.45). Very sadly, I would have to buy the £286.45 one because the other one cannot ship to where I live, so I have to save up £37.72 extra, Here is the link.
